HOW TO MOUNT THE DRIVES

According to: http://cagewebdev.com/raspberry-pi-connecting-to-a-network-drive/
and: 


IN /etc/fstab add:
    //192.168.2.29 /home/logan/Desktop/Recordings cifs user=Viper,password=' ' ,file_mode=0777,dir_mode=0777 0 0 

    reboot and the folder should be mounted

MANUALLY ADDING
    sudo mount.cifs -v '//192.168.2.29/0. Logan Backup Files/Rpi3B_CameraFeed' /home/logan/Desktop/Recordings --verbose -o user=Viper,password=' ',file_mode=0777,dir_mode=0777
